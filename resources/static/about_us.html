About the team FutureCurves

FutureCurves focuses on the mental well-being of people in isolation during the COVID-19 outbreak. It offers wholesome daily tasks for the users to finish, while getting points for redeeming coupons. An abundance of minigames to play is also a feature in our project. We first came up with the idea of having such an app to cheer people up when we found most of our peers are feeling down in the isolation caused by the COVID-19 pandemic. Also we are concerned about the affected economy as well, so we think of a win-win mechanism of helping both the users as well as businesses.
